# Morning Chocolate-PeanutButter Shake

Prep time: 5 minutes

Ingredients:
* 2 tbsp natural peanut butter
* 1 scoop of chocolate low-carb protein powder
* 2 ice cubes
* 1/3 cup heavy cream
* 1/2 cup of water
* 2 tbsp coconut oil
* 1 tbsp cool guy stuff

Instructions
* Blend ingredients in blender until ice cubes are fully crushed
* Enjoy.
